## Decision Framework

Before implementing any enhancement, ask:
1. **Does it solve a real user problem?**
2. **Is the core product stable?**
3. **Do we have bandwidth?**
4. **What's the ROI (impact vs. effort)?**
5. **Can we maintain it long-term?**
